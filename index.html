Thomas Lyle Burton - TEST

A tribute to a father who tragically lost his life in a boating accident

Tom in his early twenties enjoying a cold beer which he enjoyed along with fishing, sports spectating, gardening and family


A Timeline of major events in Tom's life:

  • 1947 - Born 18th September, second born of identical twins
  • 1946 -> 1956 - Grew up in Pambula the far South Coast of NSW before moving to Wollongong(Woonona) at the age of 10
  • 1956 -> 1963 - Attended Corrimal primary and high school graduating year 12
  • 1966 - Started working at Wollongong Steel Works which sponsored his Diploma of Metallurgy studies
  • 1969 - Conscripted to Vietnam war however exempted due to his metallurgy skills.
  • 1970 - Volunteered to attend the Vietnam War in place of his identical brother
  • 1973 - Married his first wife Michele Chapman
  • 1977 & 1979 - Had two Children to Michele - Heath and Kareena
  • 1981 -> 1982 - Seperated from Michele and divorced a year later
  • 1987 & 1989 - Had two children with Josie Shulz- Geoffrey and Murray
  • 1991 - Married Josie Shultz
  • 2000 - Tragically drowned off the coast of Bendalong whilst on a Bellambi Social Fishing Club trip

I had the privilege of a one on one conversation with my father just before his passing. We talked for over an hour were all he talked about was his children and their achievements, he was very proud of them all. RIP Dad- Heath Burton
